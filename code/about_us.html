About Us 🎷

Swiftie is a student project created to offer a simple and easy-to-use platform for music streaming.

Swiftie allows users to:

  • Create and listen to their own playlists
  • Discover new music and favorite artists
  • Enjoy a clean and simple design

Our mission is to create software that is easy to use and understand — for both older users and busy people.

Why Did We Create Swiftie?

We wanted to make something different and practical — not just a project for grades, but an app that looks and feels like a real product.

Our Team 👨‍💻👩‍💻

Swiftie was created by students interested in programming, design, and music. Each of us contributed ideas, code, and creativity.
