Introduction

This website values and respects your privacy. We are committed to protecting it by adhering to a simple principle: we do not collect any personal data from our users.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or contact details.
  • We do not use cookies or other tracking technologies.
  • We do not log IP addresses, browser types, or other usage data.
  • We do not use third-party analytics services.
  • We do not share or sell any user data because we do not collect any to begin with.

Third-Party Content

Our website contains links from third-party platforms, specifically:

  • YouTube
  • Spotify

These platforms may collect data such as your IP address, device type, browser information, or interaction behavior when you click on or interact with their content - or even just load an embedded element. This data is handled in accordance with their own privacy policies.

We do not control how these third parties handle your information. For more information, please review their privacy policies:

  • YouTube Privacy-Policy
  • Spotify Privacy-Policy

Changes to This Policy

If we ever begin collecting any type of information, we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly. Any changes will be clearly posted on this page with a new effective date.
