1. Introduction

Welcome to Swiftie. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the information you share with us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what your rights are.

2. What Information We Collect

We may collect personal and non-personal information to enhance the user experience:

  • Usage Data: information on how you use the app.
  • Device Information: type of device, operating system, browser.
  • Personal Information: in the future, we may collect usernames or emails.

3. How We Use the Information

Your data helps us improve the app, fix bugs, and provide a better music experience:

  • Enhancing features and personalizing the experience.
  • Monitoring app performance.
  • Generating anonymous statistics for school purposes.

4. Cookies and Tracking

Our site may use cookies to save settings or track anonymous usage. You can disable them in your browser, but some features may not work properly.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share or sell your data to third parties. The information is used only to improve the app as part of an educational project.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information. This is a student project, so it does not have corporate-level security.

7. Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children under this age.

8. Your Rights

  • Know what data we collect (described above).
  • Request deletion of your data (if we store personal information).
  • Refuse the use of non-essential cookies and tracking.

9. Changes to This Policy

The policy may be updated as needed. We will post the new version with an updated date.
